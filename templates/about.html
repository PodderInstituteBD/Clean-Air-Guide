Our Mission

Clean Air Guide is a simple awareness web app created for the NASA Space Apps Challenge. Our goal is to inspire people to take small but meaningful actions to reduce air pollution and protect our planet.

How It Helps

  • Encourages eco-friendly daily habits 🌱
  • Spreads awareness about pollution control 🚭
  • Motivates users through a daily checklist ✅

Team Info

Future Ideas

In the next version, we plan to include live air quality data and AI-powered pollution prediction models.
